About Us

Welcome to Yirgacheffe Coffee Farmers Cooperative Union (YCFCU)

At YCFCU, we are proud to represent the passion, dedication, and heritage of over 40,000 small-scale coffee farmers in the Yirgacheffe region of Ethiopia.

Established in 2002, our mission is to bring the world a taste of Ethiopia's finest coffee while uplifting our farmers and preserving the traditions of sustainable farming.

Yirgacheffe Coffee is globally celebrated for its unique floral and citrus flavors, grown in the highlands of southern Ethiopia. These distinct qualities stem from generations of expertise, fertile soils, and ideal climatic conditions.

Experience the story of Yirgacheffe Coffee, where every sip supports our farmers, preserves the environment, and shares the rich culture of Ethiopia with the world.

Mr. Erkehun Woldegiorgis General Manager

Natural – Sun Dried

Naturally processed beans have a heavy fruit and wine flavor. They may have hints of cherry, grape, lime, and peach. Their natural process gives them an earthy taste. If beans aren’t dried correctly, they develop a sour taste and become too brittle to roast. Yirgacheffe union provides sun dried coffee based on their need.

Read More

Washed

Instead of being picked and dried immediately, wet processing requires thorough washing. Once clean, the beans sit on rotating beds for 48 to 72 hours. This dries the entire bean evenly. Wet processing removes the traditional fermented yirgacheffe flavors and creates a lighter bodied coffee bean with citrus and floral notes.

Read More

Roasted

Medium Roasts are most commonly used for Yirgacheffe Coffee beans. This enhances its sweet aromas, smell and bright acidity. Yirgacheffe beans are small and need to be roasted delicately and carefully. Yirgacheffe union proudly provides Light, Medium, and Dark roasted coffee for both local and international markets.
